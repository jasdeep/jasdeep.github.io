Welcome! My name is Jasdeep Singh. I am a software engineer, a translator, and an aspiring researcher of humanities and social sciences.

I am currently a Associate Lead Software Engineer at Basware Corporation's Chandigarh office on the Research and Development Team, where I work among a group of leading software engineers and architects on the our core e Invoicing system.

I have also worked as translator/additional dialogue writer for three award winning Punjabi films. I curate a blog on translated Punjabi poetry. I have presented on Punjabi poetry and films in some academic conferences.
